Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Digital Detox Game ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information. Your data stays on your device.

2. Information We Collect

Digital Detox Game is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or address books
  • Photos, videos, or media files
  • Browsing history or search queries

All app data (focus session history, pet progress, coin balance, settings) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and is never transmitted to external servers.

3. In-App Purchases

Our App offers optional in-app purchases through Google Play Billing. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not collect or store any payment information, credit card details, or billing addresses.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services — for app distribution and in-app purchases
  • RevenueCat — for managing in-app purchase subscriptions (anonymous usage only, no personal data collected)
  • Expo / React Native — the development framework used to build the App

These services may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more details.

5. Children's Privacy

Digital Detox Game is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, there is no risk of children's data being compromised.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data is managed by your device's built-in security features (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date to benefit from the latest security patches.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
